About WatchParty

WatchParty is a platform that allows you to watch videos together with your friends online, no matter where they are. Simply create a room, invite your friends, and enjoy watching your favorite movies, TV shows, or any other videos in sync.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring people closer together through shared entertainment experiences. We believe that watching videos with friends should be easy and accessible to everyone, regardless of location or device.
